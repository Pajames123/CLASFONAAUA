Official Address

Message from the President

Shalom to you in the precious name of our Lord Jesus Christ.

It is with a heart full of gratitude and divine purpose that I welcome you to the official digital portal of the Christian Law Students’ Fellowship of Nigeria (CLASFON), Adekunle Ajasin University Chapter.

At CLASFON AAUA, we believe that the legal profession is not merely a career path or a means of livelihood; it is a high calling and a sacred ministry. We are a generation of students who have recognized that the "Temple of Justice" requires ministers who are not only learned in the laws of the land but are also governed by the laws of the Kingdom of God. Our mandate is clear: to raise a "New Network of Lawyers"—men and women who will stand as pillars of integrity in a world where the lines of justice are often blurred.

As law students in a rigorous academic environment like AAUA, it is easy to become overwhelmed by the weight of statutes, cases, and examinations. However, CLASFON exists to remind you that you are first a citizen of Zion before you are a student of the Law. We provide a sanctuary where your spirit is nourished even as your intellect is sharpened. We believe that Academic Excellence is a form of worship, and we strive to represent Christ by being at the forefront of our classes, law clinics, and moot court competitions.

Our fellowship is a family. It is a place where we "iron sharpeneth iron," supporting one another through the spiritual battles of campus life and the intellectual demands of the Faculty of Law. Here, we don’t just study for LL.B; we prepare for a lifetime of advocacy for the voiceless, following the footsteps of the Greatest Advocate, Jesus Christ. Let us build together, pray together, and study together until Christ is formed in us and reflected in our legal system. Welcome to the Family. Welcome to the New Network.

In His Vineyard,

Ajisafe Koyinsola

President, CLASFON AAUA

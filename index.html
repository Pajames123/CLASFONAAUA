A Message from the President

Shalom to you in the precious name of our Lord Jesus Christ.

At CLASFON AAUA, we believe that the legal profession is not merely a career path; it is a high calling and a sacred ministry. We are a generation of students who have recognized that the "Temple of Justice" requires ministers who are governed by the laws of the Kingdom of God.

The Essence of Our Identity

CLASFON AAUA Chapter is a specialized organ of the body of Christ positioned within the Faculty of Law. We are fully students of the law and fully disciples of Christ.

Our Roots and Foundation

What started as a small gathering under the trees has grown into an influential, Spirit-led organization. Our history is a testament to the faithfulness of God bridging the gap between faith and the legal profession.

The Four-Fold Mandate

1. To Enthrone Christ: Ensuring every student has a personal relationship with Jesus.
2. To Promote Academic Distinction: Fostering high intellectual standards.
3. To Foster Professional Integrity: Building character early.
4. To Provide Support: Ensuring no student falls through the cracks.

Our Core DNA: The Priest-Advocate

We are "Priest-Advocates." Before we plead before a secular judge, we stand before the Judge of all the Earth in prayer. We believe a Christian law student should be the most hardworking and intellectually curious.

A Covenant of Brotherhood

Beyond academia, we are a family. We provide a "city of refuge" where senior students mentor juniors. We share books, meals, and burdens.

A Voice for the Voiceless

We see ourselves as future defenders of the oppressed and judges who will not take bribes, crafting statutes that reflect the righteousness of God.

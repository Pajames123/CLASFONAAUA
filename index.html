Message from the President

Shalom to you in the precious name of our Lord Jesus Christ.

It is with a heart full of gratitude and divine purpose that I welcome you to the official digital portal of the Christian Law Students’ Fellowship of Nigeria (CLASFON), Adekunle Ajasin University Chapter.

At CLASFON AAUA, we believe that the legal profession is not merely a career path or a means of livelihood; it is a high calling and a sacred ministry. We are a generation of students who have recognized that the "Temple of Justice" requires ministers who are not only learned in the laws of the land but are also governed by the laws of the Kingdom of God.

Our fellowship is a family. It is a place where we "iron sharpeneth iron," supporting one another through the spiritual battles of campus life and the intellectual demands of the Faculty of Law. Here, we don’t just study for LL.B; we prepare for a lifetime of advocacy for the voiceless, following the footsteps of the Greatest Advocate, Jesus Christ.

In His Vineyard,

Ajisafe Koyinsola

President, CLASFON AAUA

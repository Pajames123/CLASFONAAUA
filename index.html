Presidential Welcome

Ajisafe Koyinsola | 2025/2026 Session

S

halom to you in the precious name of our Lord Jesus Christ. It is with a heart full of gratitude and divine purpose that I welcome you to the official digital portal of the Christian Law Students’ Fellowship of Nigeria (CLASFON), Adekunle Ajasin University Chapter.

At CLASFON AAUA, we believe that the legal profession is not merely a career path or a means of livelihood; it is a high calling and a sacred ministry. We are raising a "New Network of Lawyers" governed by the laws of the Kingdom of God. In a world where the lines of justice are often blurred, we stand as a beacon of integrity, excellence, and spiritual fervor.

Our mandate is clear: to integrate our legal training with our spiritual convictions, producing advocates who are not only learned in the law of the land but are deeply rooted in the Law of the Spirit of Life in Christ Jesus. We exist to provide a sanctuary where your faith is not compromised by your studies, but rather strengthened by it.

We are established upon the Advocate, and in Him, our victory is certain.

Shalom to you in the precious name of our Lord Jesus Christ.

It is with a heart full of gratitude and divine purpose that I welcome you to the official digital portal of the Christian Law Students’ Fellowship of Nigeria (CLASFON), Adekunle Ajasin University Chapter.

At CLASFON AAUA, we believe that the legal profession is not merely a career path; it is a high calling and a sacred ministry. Our mandate is clear: to raise a "New Network of Lawyers"—men and women who will stand as pillars of integrity governed by the laws of the Kingdom of God.

Ajisafe Koyinsola
